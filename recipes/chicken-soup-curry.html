Chicken Soup Curry

Description

A simple recipe from Reade Grandma that makes a chicken curry baked in the oven for at least 90 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 raw chicken pieces
  • 2 cans of Heinz cream of mushroom soup
  • 3-5 teasp Madras curry paste (depending how spicy you want it to be)
  • 25g basmati rice per person
  • 100g frozen peas

Steps

  1. Put the oven on 180 degrees
  2. If the chicken pieces have skin on them, fry them first, skin down. Just enough to brown the skin.
  3. Heat the Madras paste in a pan on the hob
  4. Add the soup and heat until starting to bubble
  5. Transfer to an oven proof casserole dish and add the chicken pieces
  6. Put in oven and cook for 2 hours (90 minutes if no bones in chicken)
  7. Towards the end of the curry cooking, boil the rice and peas.
  8. Serve as a ring of rice and peas mixed together and curry in the middle as it is quite a runny curry
