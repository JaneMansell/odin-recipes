Mexican Rice

Description

A quick recipe to use up leftover chicken.

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken chopped into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pepper, chopped
  • 6 mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 tblsp olive oil
  • 8cm piece of chorizo cut into small pieces
  • 2 packets of Uncle Ben's spicy mexican rice

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok or frying pan
  2. Fry the onion for 5 mins
  3. Add the pepper, mushrooms and chorizo and fry for another 5 mins
  4. Add the cooked chicken and cook long enough to reheat chicken
  5. Microwave rice according to packet instructions
  6. Stir rice into pan and serve
